Bringing home health into the future

AlayaCare is a provider of revolutionary cloud-based home health care software. With an end-to-end solution spanning clinical documentation, back-office functionality, client and family portals, and mobile care worker functionality, we offer a platform for agencies to propel towards innovation and home care of the future. We are driven to enable the care we want our loved ones to receive in the place they call home.

Our Mission

To empower care providers to achieve better health outcomes by delivering transformative technology and data insights to focus on what really matters.

Our Vision

The future of community care is connected and part of a continuum, delivered through traditional and emerging modes of care. AlayaCare makes that personalized and predictive.

Our team

We love our people. Every single member of our close-knit team is committed to building the best product possible to change the home health care industry. To achieve this ambitious goal, we believe hard work and a great attitude leads to great results, and better outcomes for our clients.
